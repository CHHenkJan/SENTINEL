Statistical Context

Mean reversion strategies have well-documented performance characteristics. They tend to perform well in ranging and volatile markets, where price oscillations around the mean are frequent and pronounced.


However, during sustained trending phases (strong bull or bear markets), mean reversion signals can produce consecutive losses as prices continue to deviate from historical averages. SENTINEL's time-based exit (5-day maximum) and hard stop loss (2%) are designed to limit drawdown during these periods.


No strategy works in all market conditions. SENTINEL is designed for specific market regimes and includes guidance on when to pause trading.
